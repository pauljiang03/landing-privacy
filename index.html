Privacy Policy

Last updated: June 20, 2026

This Privacy Policy describes how Landing (“Landing”) handles personal information in connection with the Landing mobile application and related services (the “Service”). Landing is an application for meeting and making platonic friends. This policy explains what information the Service collects, how that information is used and shared, and the rights and choices available to you. By creating an account or using the Service, you acknowledge the practices described here.

1. Information Collected

Information you provide

  • Account and verification data: email address and/or phone number, used to create your account, sign you in, and confirm that you are a real person.
  • Profile information: display name, age, photographs, prompt questions and answers, biography, and interests.
  • Location you enter: the city and neighborhood you choose to display. The Service does not collect precise GPS coordinates and never shares neighborhood-level location with other members beyond what you choose to display.
  • Content and communications: messages you send, prompt responses, and content you submit to communities, plans, or the AI concierge.

Information collected automatically

  • Usage activity: profiles you view, connections you make, and the communities and plans you join or host.
  • Device and log data: device type, operating system, app version, and diagnostic or crash information used to maintain security and reliability.

2. How Information Is Used

  • To create, display, and maintain your profile and account.
  • To suggest and facilitate friend connections, communities, and plans.
  • To verify accounts and operate trust-and-safety measures, including moderation and the prevention of fraud, spam, and abuse.
  • To provide customer support and respond to inquiries.
  • To operate, analyze, maintain, and improve the Service.
  • To comply with legal obligations and enforce applicable terms.

3. AI Concierge Feature

The Service offers an optional AI concierge that suggests a compatible group of friends. When you use this feature, the profile information and messages you share in that conversation are processed by Anthropic, PBC (the “Claude” API) to generate suggestions. This information is used solely to provide the feature and is not used by Anthropic to train its models. You are not required to use this feature.

6. Data Retention

Personal information is retained for as long as your account remains active or as needed to provide the Service. When you delete your account, your profile and associated personal information are deleted, except where retention is required to meet legal, security, or fraud-prevention obligations or to resolve disputes.

7. Account Deletion

You may delete your account at any time from within the app, which removes your profile and personal information as described in Section 6. You may also request deletion by contacting Landing using the details in Section 12.

8. Your Rights and Choices

Depending on your location, you may have the right to access, correct, delete, or receive a copy of your personal information, to object to or restrict certain processing, and to withdraw consent. You can edit or hide most profile fields directly in the app, and you can delete your account as described above. To exercise other rights, contact Landing using the details in Section 12. Landing will not discriminate against you for exercising these rights.

California residents may request disclosure or deletion of personal information and may designate an authorized agent. Residents of the EEA and UK may lodge a complaint with their local supervisory authority.

9. Children’s Privacy

The Service is intended for adults aged 18 and older. Landing does not knowingly collect personal information from anyone under 18. If such information is discovered, it will be deleted.

10. Security

Landing uses reasonable administrative, technical, and organizational safeguards designed to protect personal information. No method of transmission or storage is completely secure, and absolute security cannot be guaranteed.

11. International Data Transfers

Personal information may be processed and stored in countries other than the one in which you reside, including the United States. Where required, appropriate safeguards are used for such transfers.
