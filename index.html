Landing Crew — Privacy Policy

Last updated: June 20, 2026

Landing Crew (“we,” “us,” or “our”) is a mobile app for making real, platonic friends in your city. This policy explains what information we collect, how we use it, and the choices you have. By using Landing Crew you agree to this policy.

Information we collect

  • Account & verification: your email address and/or phone number, used to sign in and to verify your account.
  • Profile information you provide: name, age, photos, prompt answers, bio, interests, and the city/neighborhood you choose to display.
  • Approximate location: the city/neighborhood you enter. We do not collect precise GPS location, and we never share your precise location with other users.
  • Activity in the app: people you like or connect with, messages you send, and the communities and plans you join or host.
  • Technical & log data: basic device and diagnostic information used to keep the app secure and reliable.

How we use your information

  • To create and show your profile to other members.
  • To suggest and form friend connections, communities, and plans.
  • To verify accounts and keep the community safe (moderation, abuse, and fraud prevention).
  • To operate, maintain, and improve the app.
  • To comply with legal obligations.

AI features

If you use the optional AI “Concierge” feature, the profile information you share in that conversation is processed by Anthropic’s Claude API to suggest a compatible friend group. This data is used only to provide the feature and is not used to train AI models.

How your information is shared

  • With other members: the profile details and content you choose to make visible (photos, prompts, name, approximate area).
  • With service providers that operate the app on our behalf, including Supabase (authentication, database, and photo storage) and Anthropic (AI features). They process data only to provide their services to us.
  • For legal reasons when required to comply with law or protect the rights and safety of our users.

We do not sell your personal information.

Data retention

We keep your information for as long as your account is active. When you delete your account, or request deletion, we delete your personal data except where we must retain it to meet legal or safety obligations.

Your choices & rights

  • Edit or hide profile fields at any time in the app.
  • Delete your account, which removes your profile and associated personal data.
  • Request access to or deletion of your data by contacting us.

Children

Landing Crew is intended for users 18 and older. We do not knowingly collect information from anyone under 18.

Security

We use reasonable technical and organizational measures to protect your information. No method of transmission or storage is 100% secure, but we work to safeguard your data.

Changes to this policy

We may update this policy from time to time. We will revise the “Last updated” date above when we do, and significant changes will be reflected in the app.
